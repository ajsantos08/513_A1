About Me

My name is Mari, I have been grooming dogs for 10 years now. I studied at Groomers Calgary and worked there for 6 years before deciding to start my own business. I have training in various dog breeds but specialize in the grooming of Nova Scotia Duck Tolling Retrievers. My own dog Maple is my inspiration, and the reason I started grooming 10 years ago!

Size Guide

Size Guide Weight Example Breed
Small Up to 25 Lbs. Maltese, Shih Tzu, Toy Poodle
Medium 25-50 Lbs. Mini Schnauzer, American Eskimo, Cocker Spaniel
Large 50-75 Lbs. Retrievers, Collie's, Shepherds
Extra Large 75 Lbs. & Up Newfoundlander's, Bernese Mountain Dog, Great Pyrenees

Note: Due to the variance of weight/size & coat types, Doodles can fall into any one of these price ranges. Please keep in mind that prices may vary due to coat condition (matting, shedding, undercoat build-up).
